App Overview:
Slope utilizes a simple control scheme. You tilt the platform left or right by using your arrow keys or your left/right mouse clicks. This, in turn, guides your ball, keeping it balanced and preventing it from falling off the slope. Here though, because it is a phone app we'll turn left and right using the phone rotation.
